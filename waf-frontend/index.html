About TRATA

TRATA is a next-generation Transformer-based Web Application Firewall (WAF) developed by students of Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT).

Traditional firewalls rely on static rules, but TRATA leverages a Transformer-based architecture to treat HTTP requests like natural language. By analyzing contextual intent, it identifies previously unseen threats with high accuracy and low false positives.
